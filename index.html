& lt; entity*:

When I type the 3 characters above together (without the space I put there just to make it visible here) they make this symbol: <

Always with a semicolon at the end.

& gt; entity*:

When I type the 3 characters above together (without the space I put there just to make it visible here) they make this symbol: >

Always with a semicolon at the end.

* What's an ENTITY?

"An HTML entity is used to display invisible characters and reserved characters that would otherwise be interpreted as HTML code.

It is a piece of text, or string, that begins with an ampersand ( & ) and ends with a semicolon ( ; ).

HTML will interpret the less-than sign ( < ) as a tag". (https://www.educative.io/edpresso/what-are-html-entities).
